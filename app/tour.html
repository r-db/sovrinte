Mesh

Your party, mapped — without a tower.

Every Sovrinte phone within range shows up automatically. Live position when they're close, last-known pin when they've wandered out of range. Tap a name, get a heading to walk back to them.

When the party spreads out, the mesh chains — each phone relays the last position of every other phone it has ever met. See the mesh docs for the full grid logic.
