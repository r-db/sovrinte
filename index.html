What Sovrinte does

Five capabilities. Zero dependencies on someone else's server.

01 — Partner

An AI that lives on your phone

A purpose-built intelligence that lives on your device. No metering, no surveillance, no monthly bill. You ask, it answers — in the field, on the water, under the stars, with no bars of signal.

02 — Database

A library that fits in your pocket

Edible plants. Water purification. Shelter, fire, tools. Sailing basics. Navigation by sun and stars. Fishing and hunting — what's safe to eat, what isn't, what'll kill you. Curated, offline, ready before you need it.

03 — Mesh comms

Talk to your people when there's no signal

Text and voice over Bluetooth and your phone's radio. Walkie-talkie when you're near. Store the last known location of everyone you care about, and navigate to it. When two Sovrinte units come into range, they find each other automatically.

04 — Navigation

Where you are. Where they were. How to get there.

Offline maps. Sun-and-star navigation taught and assisted. Your route logged. Last-seen pins for every person in your party. The day the network goes down is the day Sovrinte is exactly the same as it was the day before.

05 — Journal

The adventure remembered, on your terms

Record what happened, where, with whom. Voice notes, photos, location. Share with your team when you reconnect — to each other or to the network. Your journal stays on your device unless you choose to send it. Memory that doesn't get monetized.

Plain answers

Things you'd ask before paying.

Does it actually work without internet?

Yes. That's the whole point. The AI model and the database both live on your device. The first time you open Sovrinte it downloads everything; after that, airplane mode is a feature, not a failure mode.

What phone do I need?

iPhone 14 or newer at launch. Sovrinte is tuned for on-device acceleration — older devices technically work, newer ones feel instant. Android (Pixel 7+, Galaxy S23+) follows in version 2.

Is the mesh comms regulated?

Bluetooth mesh works anywhere. Radio modes operate in license-free bands. We'll publish the full spec in the docs so you can verify against your local regulations before relying on it for anything critical.

Will it replace my satellite messenger?

No. A satellite messenger reaches places Sovrinte can't — true backcountry SOS belongs to inReach, ZOLEO, Bivy Stick, that class of device. Sovrinte does what those can't: a partner for thinking, deciding, and identifying, plus mesh comms with your nearby team. Carry both.

What about my data?

It's on your phone. We don't have a copy. We don't want a copy. If you opt into telemetry (you'll be asked, default off) we receive crash reports and nothing else.

Who built this?

A small team that already builds AI persistence infrastructure for production use. The same engineering discipline that keeps a synthetic intelligence accumulating memory across sessions is what makes Sovrinte run reliably without a network. We'll publish the docs that explain what — not how — once the first pre-orders close.
